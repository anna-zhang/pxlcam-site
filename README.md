# PXLCAM Site

Landing page for [PXLCAM](https://pxlcam.anna-zhang.com), a lo-fi camera app.

## About

PXLCAM captures moments in deliberate low resolution with retro effects. Every shot is stamped with a countdown to the climate deadline — a reminder of the beauty of the world as it is now, worth capturing, worth protecting.

## Pages

- `/` — Landing page with concept and photo gallery
- `/support` — Contact info and FAQs
- `/privacy` — Privacy policy
