// PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: 03/19/2026

This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and what that means for you.

Your photos

PXLCAM does not collect, upload, or have access to your photos. Everything you capture stays on your device.

Analytics, crash reporting, and beta testing

PXLCAM may use standard analytics and crash reporting tools to understand how the app is used and to fix problems when they occur. This data is anonymous and cannot be used to identify you personally.

During beta testing, the app is distributed via Apple's TestFlight platform. TestFlight may collect additional data including device information, usage data, and any feedback you choose to submit.

The Climate Clock

To display the climate countdown, PXLCAM fetches live data from the Climate Clock API. This request does not include any information about you or your device.

Email signup

If you sign up for updates, your email address is stored and used only to send updates about PXLCAM and projects by Anna Zhang.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Get in touch via email.
